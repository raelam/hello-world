How to use this site:

This site offers a database updated daily of what bills are where in the Texas Legislature. You can search the bills by committee, keyword, etc.

How to take action:

  • Don't attempt to follow all the bills, just find the ones you think are important.
    • You're going to have to do some research...
  • Go to Texas Legislature Online to sign up for updates on certain bills, topics, etc.
  • Follow the committees and reach out to committee chairs AND your representatives
  • Once the bill reaches the floor, it's much harder to defeat
  • Plan a high visibility event on days when the bills go to a hearing
  • Sign up to testify on the floor and tell your story for or against the bill

How a bill gets passed*

A bill can originate in the Sentae or the House
It is then assigned to a committee
If it passes through committee, it goes to the floor for a vote
If it passes on the floor, it goes to the other chamber
And the process starts over again the the other chamber

*This is a super simplified overview, checkout how a bill becomes a law for more information
